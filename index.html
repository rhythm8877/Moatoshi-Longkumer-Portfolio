About

Moatoshi Longkumer is a dedicated public servant and agriculturist with an M.Sc. in Agriculture from NEHU Medziphema. He represents Dimapur-II in the Nagaland Legislative Assembly and is passionate about sustainable development, youth empowerment, and public service.

With deep roots in Nagaland's agricultural heritage and a vision for modern development, Moatoshi bridges traditional wisdom with contemporary solutions. His commitment to his constituents and the state of Nagaland drives his every initiative, from grassroots community programs to legislative advocacy.

As a leader who believes in the power of education and skill development, he continues to champion causes that create lasting positive change in the lives of the people he serves.

Political Journey

2013
First Political Contest
Contested from Alongtaki constituency as an Independent candidate, marking the beginning of his political journey with a focus on grassroots development.
2018
Elected MLA (NPF)
Successfully elected as MLA from Dimapur-II constituency under the Naga People's Front (NPF), beginning his dedicated service to the people of Dimapur.
2023
Re-elected MLA (NDPP)
Re-elected as MLA from Dimapur-II under the Nationalist Democratic Progressive Party (NDPP), continuing his mission of development and progress.

Achievements & Contributions

Skill Development & ITI Reforms
Spearheaded comprehensive reforms in Industrial Training Institutes, enhancing skill development programs to meet modern industry demands.
Mobile Skill Morung Initiative
Launched innovative mobile skill training programs bringing education and skill development directly to rural communities.
Youth Employment Programs
Created sustainable employment opportunities for youth through various government and private sector partnerships.
Infrastructure Development
